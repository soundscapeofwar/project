The Soundscape of War

A summer school project exploring sounds and trauma in the full scale invasion of Ukraine through a series of interviews conducted during the summer of 2023.
Explore the video interviews

Two breathing excercises (audio in Ukrainian language only) are made available to visitors to deal with the trauma they might experience while exploring the website.

Visitors can further explore the topic by scrolling to a collection of related Ukrainian resources.

About the authors of the project

The project "Soundscape of War" is the result of 4 students, two from Ukraine, one from Germany and one from the USA, which took part at the Summer School "Witnessing and Documenting Wartime Experiences: The Current Russian War in Ukraine, Historical Perspectives, and Global Alliances" in 2023. For questions and inquiries: levytska.work@gmail.com
