About the authors and the project

The project "Soundscape of War" is the result of the work of 4 students, two from Ukraine, one from Germany and one from the USA, which took part at the Summer School "Witnessing and Documenting Wartime Experiences: The Current Russian War in Ukraine, Historical Perspectives, and Global Alliances" in 2023 at the Saarland University.
